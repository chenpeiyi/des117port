Type Story Website

Type Story is a website that introduces the viewer to the past, present, and future of typography. After drawing thumbnail sketches and wireframes, I used Illustrator to make three mockups. This is the first website that I coded with CSS and HTML! The coding process was tedious but rewarding. Background image credit: unsplash.com.

Minimalist Tumblr Template

This is a Minimalist Tumblr template. It aims to give the audience a clean interface where they can browse their content with much breathing space. It was tricky to work with many Tumblr Variables to put the content in, but the process was fun and I would definitely make more templates in the future. I worked with HTML and CSS to make the template. Background image credit: unsplash.com.

Design and the Play Instict

Design and the Play Instinct is an article written by Paul Rand. I constructed the article into a small book by designing the layout, arranging the content, and binding the book. I had lots of fun in the design process as I experimented with different color schemes and typefaces and familiarized with InDesign.
